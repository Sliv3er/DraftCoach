# League of Legends Patch 26.5 Notes

Welcome to Patch 26.5! We are making some big changes to a few champions to shake up the meta.

### Darius
Darius has been struggling to find lethal in the early game. We are significantly increasing his upfront burst damage on his Q and W, and reducing his sustained healing.
- Q Base Damage increased from 50 to 80.
- W Scaling increased.
- Q Healing reduced from 15% to 10% missing health.

### Janna
Janna is a bit too aggressive in lane right now. We are heavily nerfing her W poke damage but drastically increasing the shield amount on her E to force her into a pure peel role.
- W Damage reduced by 40%.
- E Shield increased by 30%.
- R Healing increased by 20%.

### Sion
Sion's splitpushing power is completely out of control. We are nerfing his damage to towers but buffing his base defensive stats so he can perform better in 5v5 teamfights as a pure frontline tank.
- Damage to towers reduced by 50%.
- Base Armor and Magic Resist increased by 10.
- W Passive health scaling increased.
